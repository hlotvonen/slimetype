ANCIENT CULTURES TOOK ALPHABETS FROM NEIGHBORING CIVILIZATIONS MODIFYING THEM TO FIT THEIR LINGUISTIC NEEDS AND PREFERENCES AND SO THE MULTITUDES OF ALPHABETS OF THE WORLD WERE BORN MOVING THROUGH THE NATURAL PROGRESSION OF ALPHABETS AND LANGUAGE EVOLUTION WE CONSIDERED THE BLOB ITS PRIMITIVE CIVILIZATION ON THE VERGE OF CULTURAL AND LINGUISTIC DEVELOPMENTS BY GIVING SLIME MOLD THE BUILDING BLOCKS OF THE ENGLISH CHARACTER SET WE WILL OBSERVE THE CREATION AND EVOLUTION OF THEIR LANGUAGE PATTERNS AND ALPHABETS AS WE ONCE DID IN THE PAST AS HUMAN BEINGS
THE SLIME MOLD PHYSARUM POLYCEPHALUM IS A FREE LIVING ACELLULAR SINGLE CELLED ORGANISM AND A MEMBER OF THE MYXOMYCETES GROUP THEY ARE CONSIDERED INTELLIGENT AND EFFICIENT SPATIAL PROBLEM SOLVERS WHO CAN FIND THE MOST EFFICIENT WAYS TO GET TO THEIR SOURCES OF NUTRITION THE IDEA WAS TO PRESENT THE BLOB WITH THE ENGLISH LETTERS IN THE FORM OF NUTRITION AND DOCUMENT THE PATH IT UNDERGOES TO GET TO ITS FOOD SOURCE AND IN DOING SO CREATE NEW UNIQUE LETTERS DERIVED FROM THE LATIN ALPHABET RECREATING HUMAN ALPHABETIC HISTORY IN THE PRESENT TIME WITHOUT THE PREREQUISITE OF HUMAN INTELLIGENCE BUT BY DEPENDING ON THE MICROORGANISMS NONHUMAN INTELLIGENCE
